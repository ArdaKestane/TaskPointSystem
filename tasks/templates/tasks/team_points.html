Information

Every milestone has an associated weight under 100. For all the tasks in that milestone, the team and individual grades are computed as follows. Every task created during a milestone is either accepted or failed. Failure comes from either the rejection of the work by other team members, or because the due date of the task or the milestone has passed and it was not completed. The team grade then becomes the accepted points over the points of all tasks.

Once the team grade is computed, all tasks for a milestone is divided to the number of developers. This number represents the amount of contribution expected from each developer. Then, the individual grade is computed, similar to the team grade, but only the developer's tasks are taken into account. This number is then divided to the expected value. If it is over 1, then the developer gets full grade for the milestone. The final individual grade is the weighted sum of all milestone grades.

The project has a predefined values for team and individual grades. They contribute to the project grade of the developer.
